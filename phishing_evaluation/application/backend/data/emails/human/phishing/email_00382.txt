I’m writing to ask you to participate in a Survey on Sexual Assault and Sexual Misconduct. This survey, which is being conducted at 33 college campuses across the country with the coordination of the Association of American Universities, is a follow-up to a survey conducted in 2015.  
This survey will provide critical information on the extent of sexual misconduct at Brown, and guide our efforts to prevent and address sexual assault, gender-based harassment and other forms of sexual misconduct. Together with the data collected in 2015, we will be able to assess how students’ knowledge about and experience of sexual misconduct have changed over time. Your responses will help us refine our prevention and outreach efforts to dismantle social and cultural norms that allow sexual misconduct to continue...
